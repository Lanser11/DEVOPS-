Human Resource Management (HRM) is the strategic approach to managing people within an organization to maximize their effectiveness and contribution towards achieving organizational goals. It involves various functions such as recruitment, selection, training and development, performance management, compensation and benefits, employee relations, and ensuring compliance with employment laws and regulations.

The primary objectives of HRM include ensuring the right people are in the right positions, developing their skills and abilities, fostering a positive work environment, and maintaining legal and ethical standards in employment practices. Effective HRM practices contribute to employee satisfaction, retention, and overall organizational success.
